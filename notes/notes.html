MAXIM PANCHENKO

AUDIO DESIGNER

NOTES \\\

22 sept 2024

You know, when you want to listen to some piano ambient music. Maybe it will sound with a rain or some noises. And you listen to it, and listen to it, and listen... to it. Ten minutes, half an hour, hour, two, the same track with a simple ambience. It's a magic, isn't it? To fall too deep into the feeling of another world, which you create while listening.

I still forget about that not only I can feel as deep as I think. People around the world will play damn youtube video on loop to feel life. To feel what feelings goins around them. And today I discovered this feeling in The Bear. The episode was the start of the third season and was expected to be anything but what I watched.

They did the thing. They put you in the this damn "piano and rain youtube" video on loop. You watching it and it never stops, never. And you think to yourself - why did they do this? Why did they even put fades between the same track as you listening it as youtube video? And the answer is because it was the best to convey the feeling of character's past. You're into the zone, you can't escape or remove yourself from this state. Your mind is weak to it and just flows on. There is greatness here.

The Bear; Season 3, Episode 1; Thirty-five minutes of feelings and one minute of silence (Nine Inch Nails - Together)
